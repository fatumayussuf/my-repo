About us

Prestige Travel has been a leading name in the travel industry since its inception in 1988. With a commitment to providing exceptional travel experiences, we offer a wide range of services including personalized travel planning, destination management, and exclusive tour packages for both local and international destinations. Our team of experienced travel consultants is dedicated to making your travel dreams come true, ensuring every journey is memorable and hassle-free. Whether you're seeking a relaxing beach holiday, an adventurous safari, or a cultural exploration, Prestige Travel is here to guide you every step of the way. Visit us at our Nairobi office or contact us through phone or email to start planning your next adventure.
